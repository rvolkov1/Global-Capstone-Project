About this project

This project is a research project on how affordable and renewable energy can be provided to the world, specifically three countries. These countries, India, the US, and China, are infamous for their use of fossil fuels and coal. Our goal was to research them and find an achievable, sustainable, and relatively affordable solution.

The Project was made for 8th grade honors global studies over a time span of 5 weeks. The website was coded by Ross Volkov using html and css.
